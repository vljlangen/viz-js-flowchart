World’s Fastest, Funkiest, Free-est Flowchart-o-matic (Mermaid & JS Under the Hood)

Create beautiful flowcharts in seconds!

  • Type each step of your flowchart below.
  • Use blank lines to separate nodes.
  • Press Enter inside a node for multi-line text.
  • Click “Draw Flowchart” to instantly see your funky diagram!

Note: Creates single-column charts. Export multiple as SVG to combine into two-column charts in Inkscape. Ungroup repeatedly in Inkscape for precise chart editing.
